# bs-scope — MVP Scoping

Definisce scope MVP con prioritizzazione MoSCoW, anti-scope e milestone.

## Workflow

1. Leggi `brainstorm/02-problem-framing.md` e `03-market-research.md`
2. Genera tabella MoSCoW (Must/Should/Could/Won't)
3. Definisci anti-scope list con motivo
4. Definisci milestone (MVP → v0.2 → v0.3)
5. Scrivi `brainstorm/04-mvp-scope.md`
